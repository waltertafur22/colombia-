📚 Let's Learn Grammar!

🌅

Present Simple

Every Day!

We use it for things we do every day – routines and facts!

Subject + Verb (base)

* Add -s for he/she/it

☕"Colombians drink coffee."
⚽"Mateo plays football every day."
🦜"The toucan lives in the jungle."
Time words: every day always usually never
🎬

Present Continuous

Right Now!

We use it for actions happening right now!

Subject + am/is/are + Verb + ing
⚽"Mateo is playing football."
☕"The farmer is picking coffee."
💃"The girl is dancing cumbia."
Time words: now right now at the moment look!

📊 Quick Comparison

Present Simple 🌅 Present Continuous 🎬
She drinks coffee every morning. She is drinking coffee now.
The monkey climbs trees. The monkey is climbing a tree.
Sofía dances cumbia on Fridays. Sofía is dancing cumbia!

Mateo's Special Day

📍 Bogotá & a Coffee Farm, Colombia

Mateo lives in Bogotá. He goes to school every day. His family drinks hot chocolate every morning.

Today is Saturday and Mateo is very happy. He is visiting a coffee farm in the mountains. The farmer is picking coffee beans from the trees.

Mateo's friend Sofía is taking photos of the colorful toucan. The toucan lives in the jungle near the farm.

At night, Colombians eat arepas for dinner. But right now, Mateo is eating a delicious empanada! 🥟
